Defining design

Design to me is the amalgmation of Art and Functionality. Art may or may not serve a purpose. Design however, is more specific than art. Any functioning element needs to be presented with some artistic approach, which according to me is the desfinition of design. Here is an example which may help you understand Design from my perspective.

A keyboard can be designed in many ways. With Functionality as a priority, a keyboard may look like something on the lefttop with everything in perfect order and it would work. With much more artistic freedom, it can also look like something on the rightbottom and it would work as well.
A logical keyboard is something you see on your keyboard. Design is studying frequency of the keys used and placing them together. It is assigning more than one value to some keys and having a long space bar key to improve functioning.
